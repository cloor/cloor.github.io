Seungwoo Chung

I am currently working as a Research Intern at the VIG Lab at KIST, following the completion of my M.S. in Artificial Intelligence at Hanyang University, where I conducted research under the supervision of Prof. Tae Hyun Kim at VILab. My research interests include low-level computer vision problems—such as haze removal, shadow removal, and super-resolution—as well as diffusion models and protein structure prediction. I also hold a B.S. in Mathematics and Big Data Science from Hanyang University.
