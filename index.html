Seungwoo Chung [정승우]

I am an AI researcher with an M.S. in Artificial Intelligence from Hanyang University, where I was advised by Prof. Taehyun Kim at VILab. My research focused on low-level computer vision problems, such as haze removal, shadow removal, and super-resolution. I also conducted research on diffusion models and protein structure prediction. I hold a B.S. in Mathematics and Big Data Science from Hanyang University.
